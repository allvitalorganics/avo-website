Founder & CEO
I grew up between Seoul and New York. My mom made everything from scratch—banchan, soaps, tinctures. That stuck with me. I studied what goes into the things we consume every day, and most of it didn't make sense.

All Vital Organics is the parent. Underneath it: brands that each solve one problem honestly. No filler. No marketing-first formulations. Just the thing, done right.

I care about ingredients the way some people care about typography. Every single one has to earn its place.
