The Person Behind the Platform

Alex Kim is building health and wellness brands from Seoul and New York. He runs sensitive ones (supplements), Pheeew (functional food), and 008 (social experiences) -- all under All Vital Organics. Before the products, there's the research. Before the research, there's the question: does this actually work? That's where everything starts. Not with a marketing angle. Not with a trend report. With genuine curiosity about what helps people feel better, and the honesty to say when something doesn't.
